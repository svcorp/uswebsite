PM-101 Vibration Meter

Portable Vibration Meter

  • Acceleration
  • Velocity
  • Displacement

Main Features

  • Input Channel : 1 channel vibration
  • Displayed Vibration Amplitude Units
  • Frequency range 10 Hz to 10 kHz
  • Acceleration in m/s2 or mil/s2
  • Velocity in mm/s or mil/s
  • Displacement in μm /s or mil/s
  • Sensor Type : IEPE
  • Sampling Frequency 22.05 kHz
  • Input range +/- 50g , +/-5V (Peak)
  • Operating temperature range: 0 °C to 50°C
  • Storage temperature range: -20 °C to 70°C
  • Humidity : 5% ~ 95% Non-condensing
  • A/D converter : 24 bit
  • Dynamic range : 100 dB
