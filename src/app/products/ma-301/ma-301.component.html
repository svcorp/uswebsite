MA301 Low Frequency 3 Channel Accelerometer

Specifications

  • 3 Channel(X, Y, Z-Axis) MEMS sensor signal input (LEMO)
  • Typical input: ±2g, 2000mV/g, 0~400Hz range
  • Differential amplifier (x1.25 gain)
  • 1g Offset adjustment
  • +5V DC power input & Power LED
  • ±4V differential or 0.5V - 4.5V single ended outputs or 4 - 20 mA • Galvanized isolation
  • Responds to DC & AC Acceleration
  • ±2g, ±5g, ±10g, ±25g, ±50g, ±100g customized model
  • DC 9~12V or 9V battery power input
  • OKW A9406352 case + aluminum panel
  • Case Operating Temperature: -55 to +125 °C
  • Storage Temperature: -55 to +125 °C
  • Acceleration Over-range, 2000g for 0.1 ms
  • Voltage on VDD to GND: -0.5V to 6.5V
  • Voltage on Any Pin (except DV) to GND: -0.5V to VDD+0.5V
  • Vontage on DV to GND: ±15V
  • Power Dissipation: 50 mW
